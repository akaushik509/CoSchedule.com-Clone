FAQs

How do I upgrade from a Free to a Pro plan?

It’s easy! Just visit the billing page in your account. There you can select a new plan. Once you complete the billing prompts, your account will automatically upgrade to Pro & unlock all your new features.

How do I get started with Marketing Suite?

To get started, schedule your demo of the CoSchedule Marketing Suite. You’ll get 1:1 time with a CoSchedule expert to see how it can help you coordinate your team, projects, & process.

Do you offer discounts for nonprofits?

To make it easier for your nonprofit to organize all of your marketing with CoSchedule, we offer a 30% discount on all of our paid plans. Learn more.

Which payment types do you accept?

Plastic is fantastic. We accept credit card payments for all of our paid plans. Credit cards are not required for Marketing Calendar Free. We may also accept other payment types for Marketing Suite. Check with your account executive for details. For more information on billing, visit our Billing FAQs support doc.

I’m just getting started. How can I learn more about CoSchedule?

If you are using Marketing Calendar Free or Marketing Calendar Pro, check out our Getting Started Guides to learn the ins and outs of using your new calendar. If you’re in a Marketing Suite plan, you’ll get personalized onboarding & support from your dedicated customer success manager.They’ll be in touch, and they’re excited to help you & your team reach your goals.

Where can I find other helpful resources?

Our guides will show you how to get organized with your new CoSchedule calendar. Review our Power Tips for inspiration. Or visit the Actionable Marketing Institute to learn how to improve your marketing.
